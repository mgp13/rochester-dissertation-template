The author was born in India. She attended Savitribai Phule Pune University and graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Microbiology in 2014 and a Master of Science degree in Bioinformatics in 2017. She began doctoral studies in Biophysics, Structural and Computational Biology at the University of Rochester in 2017. She received the Master of Science degree in Biophysics in 2019. She pursued her research in computational biology under the direction of Professor Juilee Thakar.
